whistlr by gdakram

A twitter and last.fm mashup in AIR.

ALERT: As of 9/9/10 Whistlr is not up to date with twitter's OAuth requirement. Code update will be pushed in about a week's time from now. Sorry and thanks for your patience.

A cute little multi-lingual desktop application written in Adobe AIR that's a mashup between your twitter and last.fm accounts that makes it easy to share your music digs on last.fm with your twitter peeps.

Stay updated through twitter: @whistlr, @gdakram

Rationale behind why it's a desktop app and not web-based

It's your twitter account and you should have complete control over its password.
